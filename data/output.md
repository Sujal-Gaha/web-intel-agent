We understand that there is a skill gap between what is learnt in Colleges and what is required at the work. Most people learn the skills required in real life after they go to work. SkillPrompt founders realized there is an opportunity to solve this problem and an organization was set up to help the individuals to gain the real life skills that are required at the workplace.
